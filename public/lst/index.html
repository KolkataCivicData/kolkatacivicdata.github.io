Heat exposure across Kolkata

This map shows how hot different surfaces get across Kolkata during the pre-monsoon season (Apr–Jun). Areas shown in dark red experience significantly higher surface temperatures and may pose increased health risks for outdoor workers, while blue areas (water and green cover) stay cooler.
